# Life Review Loop

Collect observations first: meaningful events, energy, commitments, relationships, health signals, surprises, and unfinished concerns. Separate observation from interpretation. Identify at most a few themes, test them against counterexamples, then choose one reversible experiment and a review date.

Include “continue as is”, “rest”, and “stop optimizing this area” as valid outcomes. Escalate health, safety, legal, financial, or severe distress concerns to appropriate humans rather than converting them into productivity goals.
