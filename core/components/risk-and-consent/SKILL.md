# Risk and Consent Gate

Evaluate risk before triviality or pattern choice. Classify the proposed action, not only the user's topic.

| Level | Default autonomy |
|---|---|
| Low | bounded execution within stated scope |
| Medium | reversible experiment; explain uncertainty |
| High | advice only, or exact-action human confirmation |
| Critical | stop automation and escalate to immediate human support |

Consent must be informed, specific, current, reversible, and attributable to the person with authority. Silence, earlier consent, or consent to a plan is not consent to a newly changed action. Never combine several materially different actions into one confirmation.

Fail closed when risk, authority, affected people, or reversibility is unclear. Record the proposed action and confirmation decision without storing unnecessary sensitive content.
