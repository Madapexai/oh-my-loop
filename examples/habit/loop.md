# Habit: build a sustainable walking routine

Goal: learn whether a small walk improves afternoon energy without harming health or schedule. The goal is not a perfect streak.

- Cue: after lunch on weekdays.
- Tiny action: put on shoes and walk for five minutes.
- Environment: shoes visible; indoor alternative for unsafe weather.
- Observation: completion, energy before/after, pain or unusual symptoms.
- Review: after two weeks, not after every miss.

Continue, shrink, move the cue, or stop based on observations. Pain or relevant medical concerns leave this loop and go to an appropriate professional. No personal data is persisted without consent.
